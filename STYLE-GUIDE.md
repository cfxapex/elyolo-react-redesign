# 🔥 ELYOLO STYLE GUIDE - GRITTY & RAW

## Design Philosophy

**NO SOFT EDGES. NO PASTEL COLORS. JUST RAW POWER.**

This site is for **warriors**, **survivors**, and **fighters**. The design reflects that:
- Bold, aggressive typography (Impact font)
- High-contrast colors (red/black/yellow)
- Hard edges and sharp shadows
- In-your-face attitude

## Color Palette

```css
--primary-color: #ff0844    /* Hot Pink/Red - AGGRESSIVE */
--accent-color: #ffed00     /* Electric Yellow - ENERGY */
--background: #0a0a0a       /* Deep Black */
--surface: #1a1a1a          /* Dark Gray */
--text-primary: #fff        /* Pure White */
```

### What Each Color Means:
- **Red (#ff0844)** - PASSION, FIGHT, DETERMINATION
- **Yellow (#ffed00)** - ENERGY, HOPE, LIGHT IN DARKNESS
- **Black** - STRENGTH, DEPTH, NO BS

## Typography

### Headings
- Font: **Impact**, Arial Black, sans-serif
- Weight: 900 (BLACK)
- Transform: UPPERCASE
- Letter-spacing: WIDE (2-3px)
- Text-shadow: LAYERED for depth

### Body Text
- Font: Arial, sans-serif (cleaner for reading)
- Keep it REAL - short sentences, straight talk
- NO fluff, NO corporate speak

## Visual Elements

### Buttons
- **HARD EDGES** - No border-radius
- **THICK BORDERS** - 3px minimum
- **BOX SHADOWS** - Offset shadows for 3D effect
- **HOVER STATES** - Translate upward, increase shadow

### Cards/Boxes
- **BORDERS** - Always visible, always bold
- **LEFT ACCENT** - 4px colored stripe on left
- **BOX SHADOWS** - Hard shadows, never soft
- **HOVER** - Shift, don't fade

### Effects
- **Text Shadows** - Layered, colorful, aggressive
- **Scan Lines** - Subtle repeating gradient overlay
- **Grit Texture** - Diagonal stripes at low opacity

## The Vibe

### DO:
- ✅ Be BOLD
- ✅ Be DIRECT
- ✅ Use STRONG language
- ✅ Show EMOTION
- ✅ Keep it REAL
- ✅ Make it POP

### DON'T:
- ❌ Be subtle
- ❌ Use pastels
- ❌ Round corners everywhere
- ❌ Soft shadows
- ❌ Corporate/formal language
- ❌ Hide behind politeness

## Content Tone

### Voice:
- **Direct** - Say what you mean
- **Empowering** - YOU CAN DO THIS
- **Real** - No sugar-coating
- **Motivational** - But not cheesy

### Example Good Copy:
> "YOUR STORY MATTERS. Have you overcome adversity? Battled through hardship?
> Found strength you didn't know you had? SHARE IT."

### Example Bad Copy:
> "We would be delighted if you would consider sharing your journey with our community..."

## Animations

- **FAST & SNAPPY** - No slow fades
- **DIRECT MOVEMENT** - Translate, scale, rotate
- **IMPACT** - Make it felt
- **TIMING** - 0.2-0.3s max

## Accessibility

Even though we're LOUD, we still need to be:
- **Readable** - High contrast
- **Navigable** - Clear focus states
- **Inclusive** - Everyone can use it

## New Features

### Community Story Submission
- Users can share their own stories
- Option to post anonymously
- Multiple categories (disability, mental health, epilepsy, etc.)
- Privacy-first approach
- Review before publishing

## The Message

**This isn't a corporate site. This is a MOVEMENT.**

Every design choice should say:
- "You're not alone"
- "Your struggle is valid"
- "You have the power to overcome"
- "Share your truth"
- "Inspire others"
